Let's make some Chimarrão shall we?

This is a list of items you will need for preparing your own chimarrão

And I also a have included links on were to acquire them. click on the images of each item to be redirected to where you can purchase them

  • A Cuia to place your Erva Mate inside
  • A bomba to drink from
  • And the most important item of them all the Erva Mate itself

Let's make it then!

  • First add the erva mate to the cuia, just to neck of the cuia, the bit were it tapers out. Tap it a few times to help the erva-mate settle.
  • After that is done, place your hand on 2/3 of the cuia's mouth and slowly begin to till it in until it is at an angle 135º and then carefully bring the cuia back into a 45º angle.
  • Now that the cuia is resting in the 45º angle, with the most timid of water flow, under a tap or with a cup begin pouring cold or mildly warm water in to the cuia just to where the neck funnels.
  • Leave the cuia to rest in that 45º angle until ALL of the water has been absorbed.
  • As soon as all the water has been absorbed pour in hot, but not boiling, water in to the cuia just shy of the very top of the erva.
  • Gently place the bomba in the cuia, with a finger covering the hole, as close to the erva but without damaging the shaped erva, as it may cause blockage, as close to the bottom as possible.

THERE YOU GO, ENJOY IT MY FRIEND!
